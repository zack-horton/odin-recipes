Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a baked, noodle-based dish that is great for serving large groups of people at family gatherings or at holidays. To make things even better, it easily reheats in the microwave for great leftovers or you can pop it back in the oven for a more tasty leftover dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 block of cream cheese
  • 1/2 block of cottage cheese
  • 2 cans of Roa's marinara / spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded parmesean cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. In a large baking dish, alternate the layers of noodles and (sauce + cottage cheese + cream cheese)
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
  4. Sprinkle with parmesean cheese after baking (or with 5 minutes left for better melting)
